iDent is a modern and intuitive dental clinic management application designed to streamline operations and enhance patient care. Whether you manage a single clinic or a network of dental facilities, Ident provides the tools needed to organize appointments, track patient records, and optimize daily workflows.

With an easy-to-use interface, robust functionality, and secure data management, iDent is tailored to meet the needs of dentists, administrators, and staff. The application focuses on improving efficiency and ensuring a seamless experience for both practitioners and patients.

Key features of Ident include:

- Appointment scheduling and management
- Comprehensive patient record tracking
- Billing and invoicing tools
- Reporting and analytics for informed decision-making
- Secure data handling to protect patient confidentiality

Whether you're a small practice or a growing clinic, iDent is your trusted partner in delivering exceptional dental care.
